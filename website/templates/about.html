About Us


We are a group of young minds, paasionate about community development, seeking to be societal engineers and hoping to drive changes in their Region and beyond.

We belive that the realization of these changes can be done with united efforts and well-concerted partnerships and collaborations through an all-inclusive approach.

The group envisage initiating projects in the field of Education, through Educational Enhancement(EEP); Environment and Sanitation, through an Environmental Stewardship Programs(ESP); Health, through a Comprehensive Health Packs(CHP), and Agriculture, through a 3D/Modernized Agricultural Techniques(MAT).
